My top 5 movies of all time.

1. The Dark knight (2008)

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Batman is considered to be one of the most popular hero's among the DC comick, thus this movie has shown how batman can fight crime even without any super powers. Which makes it more intersting to watch.

2. The GodFather

The early life and career of Vito Corleone in 1920s New York City is portrayed, while his son, Michael, expands and tightens his grip on the family crime syndicate.

The Godfather movie is a movie based on true story which is very fasinating to watch.

3. The Lord of the Rings:The Two Towers

While Frodo and Sam edge closer to Mordor with the help of the shifty Gollum, the divided fellowship makes a stand against Sauron's new ally, Saruman, and his hordes of Isengard

Lord of the Rings consists of many fantasy that makes the movie intersting to watch

4.Suicide Squad

A secret government agency recruits some of the most dangerous incarcerated super-villains to form a defensive task force. Their first mission: save the world from the apocalypse.

Suicide Squad consists of a group of criminals that fought together to save the city, it shows how team work is essential

5.Avengers: Endgame

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe

Avengers has many science fiction adventures
